# Natural Convection Study

![Cover](cover.png)

---

# Engineering problem

Temperature differences naturally generate airflow
through buoyancy-driven convection.

Understanding this behavior is important
for passive cooling systems
and thermal airflow prediction.

---

# Analysis overview

Natural convection airflow behavior
inside a heated environment
was analyzed to observe airflow movement
and thermal circulation patterns.

---

# Key observations

• buoyancy-driven airflow  
• thermal circulation structures  
• temperature stratification  
• low velocity convection regions  

---

# Engineering relevance

Natural convection analysis helps improve:

• passive cooling systems  
• thermal management  
• airflow prediction  
• energy-efficient design
